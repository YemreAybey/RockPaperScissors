ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

Created by Yemre AYBEY

RULES

  • Everytime you hit a button computer selects rock, paper or scissors at random.
  • Every button hit is counted as round.
  • If you win a round you get a point. If you lose, computer gets a point. If it's a draw you both don't get any points.
  • After 5 rounds both of your scores will be calculated. The one who has the higher score will be the winner.
  • After that all of the scores will be reset. You can start another game by just clicking any button.
